Hi, I'm Verónica

I’m from a small town in Catalonia (Spain) but now settled in Swindon (UK).

I love creating things, from digital layouts to drawings, sewing patterns or jewellery. Amongst my many interests, my favourites are yoga, hiking, and discovering new places and their history.

I spent the first part of my professional years in a library, where I realised the importance of Information Architecture and began my transition into User Interface Design while working on the library’s websites. I have a background in information classification, handling and processing with a user-centric approach, together with great organisational skills.

I am also creative, self-driven, and I always enjoy learning.
