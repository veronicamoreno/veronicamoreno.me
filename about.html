Hi, I’m Verónica.

I breathe design in everything I do. I like drawing and painting in my spare time (it relaxes me). However, my main focus is on UI design because it allows me to combine my artistic talent with my problem-solving skills. Not long ago I went to a restaurant and there was this system by which you order and pay with a QR code. The experience was very poor. It was complicated and it had too many steps. On my way home I couldn't help thinking about how I would improve the experience if I worked for the company that created that system. I'm also a bit of a perfectionist and sometimes I pay too much attention to detail. Over time I've learned that it will never be perfect and I need to let it go to meet deadlines. I've learned to live with “good enough for now".
